#+title: special characters in Emacs
* ñ : `C-x 8 ~ n`
* tilde : `C-x 8 ' <vowel>`
  tag á é í ó ú
* « and » : `C-x 8 [<>]`
* ü : `C-x 8 " u`
* C-x 8 RET <name of char>
  does anything
* works for certain special characters
  C-x 8 <char>
