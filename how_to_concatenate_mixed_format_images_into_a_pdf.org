#+title: how to concatenate mixed-format images into a pdf
* via imagemagick (preferred)
** how
   magick x.pdf y.bmp z.png output.pdf
** to specify individual pages
   This picks just the first and fourth page:
   magic 'x.pdf[0]' 'x.pdf[3]' output.pdf
*** PITFALLs
**** The single quotes matter.
**** Counting backward *shuold* work with negative numbers, doesn't.
     There's a known ghostscript bug preventing it
     from working for me as of <2025-11-04 Tue>.
** more
   Once, it had memory and resolution problems.
   But the last time I tried it worked fine,
   joining .jpg files into a pdf.
* via pdfunite
  Part of the poppler-utils package.
  pdfunite 1.pdf 2.pdf output.pdf
