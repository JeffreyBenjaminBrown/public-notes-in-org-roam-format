#+title: In animals, boldness ~ rigidity, while shyness ~ sociability ~ creativity.
Boldness in animals is associated with rigidity -- difficulty updating their actions in response to new information.

On the other end of the spectrum, shyness, sociability and creativity are also associated in animals.
