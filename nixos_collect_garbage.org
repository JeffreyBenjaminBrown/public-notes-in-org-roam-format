  :PROPERTIES:
  :ID:       d014e570-e8da-4661-8111-01d19947e2b2
  :END:
  The sudo is important -- otherwise lots of stuff survives.

  sudo nix-collect-garbage --delete-older-than 3d
    # keep the last week
  sudo nix-collect-garbage -d
    # keep only latest config
