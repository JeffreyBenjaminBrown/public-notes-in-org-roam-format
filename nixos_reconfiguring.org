  Here the prefix is "emacsPackages":

  $ nix repl '<nixpkgs>'

  > emacsPackages.<TAB>

  Here the prefix is "system.autoUpgrade":

  [jeff@jbb-dell:~/nix/nixpkgs]$ nixos-option system.autoUpgrade

  allowReboot
  channel
  dates
  enable
  flags
  flake
  randomizedDelaySec
