#+title: Shared complaints are a conversational black hole. Beware it.
It's not necessarily bad. It can be good for bonding, and maybe even for finding a solution. But it does have a sort of gravitational pull that can be hard to escape, and should therefore be born in mind if other topics seem more likely to be fruitful.
